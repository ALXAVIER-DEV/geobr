# log history of geobr package development

-------------------------------------------------------
# geobr v1.0 (2019-30-07)

* Launch of **geobr** v1.0 on [CRAN](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/geobr/index.html) with the following data sets:
  * Country
  * States
  * Regions
  * Meso regions
  * Micro regions
  * Municipalities
  * Census weighting areas
  * Census tracts
  * Statistical Grid
